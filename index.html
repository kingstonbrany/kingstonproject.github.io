About St. Lawrence SS Sonde

St. Lawrence was a beloved saint who passed away in the late 17th century. The school was named in his honor due to his heroic acts and dedication to principles such as love, care, and Christianity, which the school also strives to uphold.

Key Features

  • Exceptional education provided by dedicated teachers
  • Supportive staff available 24/7
  • Outstanding student leadership and self-control
